Sustainability

Seven-X reduces the carbon footprint for processing and distributing a fresh milk product. The evaporators are designed to recycle the steam 200 times and that allows for economical evaporation. Further, evaporators located on the farm can utilize by-product heat from waste fermenters and thus require minimal energy inputs. In comparison, spray dry evaporation processes for milk are energy intense.

The efficiency of the process coupled with potential savings in transportation and storage makes Seven-X a sustainable cost effective milk product. With water activity below 0.88, Seven-X can be efficiently shipped unrefrigerated in lined standard containers or bag-in-box by truck or rail to distant locations.
