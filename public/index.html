Dairyvative Technology, founded by Dr. Charles E Sizer, develops, licenses, and provides service for the patented SEVENx technology for processing shelf-stable milk concentrate. This technology will enable worldwide distribution of quality fresh lactose free milk product.

The Technology

Dairyvative technology has a patented, paradigm changing new technology that allows high quality lactose free pasteurized milk to be concentrated 6 to 9 times. The resulting product is a dense but fluid lactose-free shelf stable milk with water activity less than 0.88 that can be distributed without refrigeration.
