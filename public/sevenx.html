SEVENx

The SEVENx process is a novel, patented technology that allows pasteurized milk to be concentrated to a liquid that has one seventh its original volume. SEVENx removes 95% of the original water yielding a 5.8x mass concentration that has 85% less volume than the raw milk. The technology effectively reduces the volume of seven tankers of milk into one container of concentrate.

The end product is uniquely lactose-free and shelf-stable without refrigeration for up to one year at ambient temperatures. The highly sustainable process uses moderate temperatures that are gentle enough to leave milk proteins intact; the end product is usable both as an ingredient and a concentrate, with the reconstituted product has the pleasant flavor of fresh milk.
