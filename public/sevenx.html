Seven-X

Seven-X is a patented, paradigm changing new technology that allows high quality pasteurized milk to be concentrated 6 to 9 times. The resulting product is a dense but fluid lactose-free shelf stable milk with water activity less than 0.88 that can be distributed without refrigeration.

Product from the patented process has these characteristics:

  • Volume reduced by 7 fold
  • Same flavor as pasteurized milk
  • Shelf-stable for up to six months
  • Reduced lactose content

What is Unique?

  • Milk is concentrated up to 7X
  • Product is shelf stable without exposure to high temperatures
  • Proteins are not denatured
  • Product tastes fresh when reconstituted
  • Seven-X has a reduced carbon footprint
