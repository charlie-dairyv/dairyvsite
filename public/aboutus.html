Company

Dairyvative Technologies , is a Wisconsin based dairy business with patented SEVENx technology for concentrating milk. The basis of our business is licensing and servicing the sustainable Sevenx process technology.

Vision

Our vision is to enable worldwide supply of fresh milk. We are focused on improving the global consumer experience by delivering a fresh quality, lactose-free product while furthering efficient use of earth s resources through the development of green dairy technology. To that end, we are committed to sustainable growth of the dairy industry and the development of new opportunities and markets for dairy farmers and cooperatives.

Dairyvative is dedicated to high ethical standards. We believe in providing an environment where each person is recognized for the difference he or she makes providing an environment where employees can flourish.

Leadership

Dr. Charles E Sizer, III is a internationally recognized food scientist with expertise in fluid food processing and packaging systems. A seventeen-year veteran of Tetra Pak, his efforts in the design of product and packaging sterilization processes resulted in 15 US patents and over 60 patents granted worldwide. As Director of the National Center for Food Safety and Technology at Illinois Institute of Technology, he was at the forefront of cutting-edge research and technical programs.

Dr. Sizer has held substantial professional positions and affiliations with the National Science Foundation, National Food Processors Association, Food Processor s Institute, Institute of Food Technology, Dairy and Food Industry Suppliers Association, Editorial Board of the Journal of Packaging Technology, and the Institute of Thermal Processing Specialists, Dr. Sizer has recently received the FDA Center for Food Safety and Applied Nutrition Director s Special Citation Award, and the Chicago Section of the Institute of Food Technologists Achievement Award for Outstanding Achievement in Food Science.
